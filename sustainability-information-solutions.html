Resource Blue Printing and Process Efficiency

Clients

Manufacturers that are aiming to reduce usage of resources while also aiming to increase productivity

Inter-disciplinary approach

Combines operational experience with best management practices and knowledge of latest innovative technologies.

Footprint:

India, Sri-Lanka, Bangladesh, Turkey, China

Uniqueness:

  • Data driven approach that uses measurement systems
  • Proven change management processes to ensure team alignment
  • Access to cutting edge and latest technologies
  • New management tools developed for resource efficiency such as Value Added Resources

Services offered include:

  • Resource baselining for energy, water, chemical usage and waste
  • Energy-Carbon-GHG accounting
  • Performance benchmarking
  • Techno-commercial feasibility of energy-water-waste solutions
  • Goal setting and operational implementation with on-site support
  • Vendor selection support
  • Productivity optimization using tools such as lean,
  • Systems for environmental management (EMS) and resource tracking
  • Financial linkages for long term projects
