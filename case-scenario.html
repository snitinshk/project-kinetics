Case Scenario

Developing a market entry strategy and roadmap for a global EHS training academy in India

Client context:

A leading international sustainability catalyst looking to establish a EHS training academy in India to meet the potential demand for for people with sustainability related skills in India. The objective of the academy is to identify the gaps and install capacity to bridge the same and partner with existing institutions to establish the infrastructure for roll-out.

Related practice areas:

  • Building market access and insight
  • Creating and implementing a sustainability blueprint
  • Developing Sustainability related brands and expanding market presence
  • Providing access to investments and Sustainability finance

Force multipliers deployed:

  • Building market access and insight
  • Creating and implementing a sustainability blueprint
  • Developing Sustainability related brands and expanding market presence
  • Providing access to investments and Sustainability finance

cKinetics Deliverables

Landscape assessment to identify:

  • industries where there is a demand for people
  • opportunity areas which will emerge (e.g. EHS training, Renewable Energy professionals, energy management experts etc.) and
  • talent skill and knowledge gap (in both number of people and specific skill areas)

Geographical and Sector Assessment:

The geographies/ geographical clusters where training capacity is needed.

Identifying pathways to bridge this gap:

Evaluatingoptions such as linkage and collaboration with industry and academia, training and curriculum development, sector specific technical training programs and franchising model for refresher courses.

Assessment of Potential Implementation Partners:

Potential partners and models of engagement.

Go-to-Market Strategy:

Roadmap for rollout of the Academy.
