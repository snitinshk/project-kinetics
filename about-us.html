Who We Are

cKinetics is a specialized Sustainability Advisory firm that provides end-to-end solutions for investors and businesses. With headquarters in New Delhi, India and a US office in Palo Alto, California; the company works with clients and partners globally.

We are a team of specialists across multiple disciplines who have come together to develop solutions that are sustainable for the planet and are profitable.

  • Building market access and insight
  • Creating and implementing a sustainability blueprint
  • Developing Sustainability related brands and expanding market presence
  • Providing access to investments and Sustainability finance

We strongly believe that resource efficient growth practices both on the production and consumption front will form the backdrop of the competitive environment over the next decade. Our focus is on shaping interventions to enable our client organizations, to emerge as becoming competitive and future-proof in the changing global order where natural resources are being repriced.

Our track record as specialists in leveraging cross-functional management resources across geographies uniquely positions us as a ‘partner of choice’ to design and execute profitable business models that enhance and promote Sustainability. We help architect and implement growth oriented sustainable solutions by bringing a deep understanding of sector dynamics across industries. cKinetics engagements are characterized by a demonstrated commitment to bring

Partner of choice

We work across a number of sectors such as energy (conservation, renewable and industrial efficiency), water, green chemistry, waste management, agriculture and forestation, with a focus on technology adoption and adaptation measures.

cKinetics also helps seed and build industry-shaping initiatives. Amongst the earliest one was India Carbon Outlook that was announced as a commitment by the firm in 2009 at the Clinton Global Initiative. Since then, we have helped launch several more such initiatives: some with our clients and many for our clients.
